Larkin University is a small, private university in Florida known for its close-knit academic community and commitment to preparing healthcare professionals. The Student Recruiter plays a critical role in engaging and enrolling students for the College of Pharmacy through strategic outreach, relationship-building, and event planning.
Universities

Responsibilities

Actively represent the university at high schools, community colleges, college fairs, health science expos, and other recruitment venues to attract prospective students
Conduct engaging presentations and information sessions (in-person and virtual) to highlight the College of Pharmacy’s programs, faculty expertise, research opportunities, and student support services
Build and maintain meaningful connections with prospective students, their families, school counselors, alumni, and community organizations
Serve as a primary point of contact for prospective pharmacy students, providing personalized support and guidance throughout the admissions process
Create and implement strategic initiatives to identify and nurture a pipeline of future pharmacy students, starting from middle and high school engagement to pre-pharmacy undergraduate pathways
Partner with science educators, career advisors, pre-health clubs, and pharmacy organizations to create early exposure opportunities for students interested in pharmacy careers
Develop community partnerships, mentoring programs, and outreach events to inspire interest in pharmacy education and strengthen long-term recruitment efforts
Plan and coordinate on-campus and virtual recruitment events, including open houses, information sessions, preview days, and campus tours
Collaborate with faculty, staff, and current students to create dynamic and memorable experiences for prospective students and their families
Represent the College of Pharmacy at professional and community events to elevate program visibility
Work closely with the Senior Director of Admissions to develop and execute data-driven recruitment strategies aligned with institutional goals
Collaborate with marketing teams to design compelling recruitment materials, social media campaigns, and digital content to engage prospective students
Provide market feedback and trend analysis to inform program promotion strategies
Maintain accurate records of outreach activities, prospective student engagement, and application progress in CRM and student information systems
Monitor key performance metrics to track progress toward enrollment targets and pipeline development initiatives
Assist with admissions-related administrative tasks, including reviewing applications and coordinating communications with prospective students
